Credit service terms

The Service terms details panel tab of the Service types page, displays basic service terms of any credit service.

 

Service terms

Description

Interest charge

Interest rate

The interest rate for credit

Interest charge period

Interest period. Calculated in accordance with the Bank requirements

Forced interest charge period

The frequency with which the interest should be reflected in the accounts. This is in addition to events when the interest is reflected in connection with any credit event (the end of the billing period, payment, etc.). The period is calculated taking weekends and holidays into account.

Invoicing

Invoicing period

Settlement period (the period of invoicing, billing date). Can be calculated by weekends and holidays taking into account. Start date of the further billing period is the date next to the previous billing period expiration date.

Mandatory amount due

Size of mandatory payment. If it is not repaid in full, outstanding part forms the overdue debt.

Mandatory amount due minimum

The boundary of the minimum payment. The minimum threshold for the mandatory payment, below which it cannot be calculated. Fixed value.

Due date period

The deadline for mandatory payments. Specified in invoices for customers.

Floating period

The period between when a transaction occurs and when it is posted to a credit card account (usually one or two days).

Grace period

Using grace period

Grace period is available. This term specifies the grace period inclusion mechanism, that is, the percent conversion (cancellation) in the case of full payment of debt in the period.

Grace period length

Length of the grace period. This term is used to calculate the end date of the grace period (a fixed number of days from the end of the calculation period). Calculated from the end of the billing period and is defined as a fixed number of days from the end of the calculation period.

Mandatory amount due tolerance

Tolerance of non-payment of the mandatory payment.

Grace period repayment condition

The condition of the grace period. Defines a set of operations and share in which they are to be repaid for the condition of the grace period.

Grace interest rate

The interest rate at which interest is recalculated if the condition of the grace period. For the complete abolition of interest rate this should be set equal to 0%.

Aging and penalties

Charge penalty if overdue

The term defines that it is necessary to charge a penalty if the mandatory payment was mot made.

Penalty rate

Amount of the fine. Sum of fines for delinquency. Calculated as the underpaid amount of mandatory payment.

Penalty charging period

Date of accrual of penalties.

Total amount due tolerance

Tolerance to fulfill the conditions of mandatory payment. Sum of the tolerance within which the customer cannot pay the required amount, but the condition of mandatory payment would be met. Set for leveling technical inconsistencies on amounts (conversion, rounding).

Repayment

Repayment priority

Priority of the debt in the queue for repayment depends on the type of transaction, date of transaction and the debt account

Use own funds

Ability to use own funds. Specifies the opportunity to use the customer’s own funds or only credit:

1.First flow of credit, and on a lack of it, own funds

2.First own funds and on a lack of it, credit ones

The difference of the values characterizes a “credit line” and “debit card with an overdraft limit”.

Repayment condition

Terms of repayment, that is, distribution rules for the payments

•No conditions — immediately upon payment

•Debt exhibited to payment — after billing period closure

•Between the date of issue and repayment date

Exceed limit

Limit value

Credit limit. Restrictive limit established for debit transactions. Used as the upper limit for the total product. The customer cannot be issued a credit limit above the amount specified in this parameter.

Provide limit fee

The fee is charged from the account for a credit limit. Fixed amount or percentage of the credit limit with minimum/maximum limits.

Change limit fee

Fee for the credit limit changing. Charged from the account for the change in the credit limit. Fixed amount or percentage of the credit limit changes with minimum/maximum limits.

Limit servicing fee

Fee for credit line servicing. Charged from the account for loan servicing. Fixed amount or percentage of the credit limit or principal debt.
