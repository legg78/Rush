Viewing customers

1.Click Customers > Customers.

2.Select a tab with the entity that corresponds to the customer type and uniquely determines it — Customer, Person, Organization, Contract, etc.

The tab selection defines specific entity parameters for searching and does not influence the search results.

3.From the Institution list, click the institution that serves the customer.

4.Set the mandatory search parameters.

To specify a customer or contract number, you can use a mask (at least 4 digits and the asterisk or percent symbols as wildcards; for example, *1234 or %1234, *1*234 or %1%234).

Note: To reduce the search time, you can specify the agent that serves the customer by selecting it from the Agent list.

5.Click Search.

The customers meeting the search criteria are displayed on the result search pane. The following tabs display different information about the customer.

Tabs

Description

Details

General information and personal data about the selected customer.

Additional

Additional specific data about the customer. To add a parameter, use the Flexible field page.

Contracts

List of contracts concluded with the customer within the scope of the institution, plus information about each of them (number, type, product, contractual time).

Cards

Cards issued to the selected customer. Card information can be viewed in a separate window.

If a customer has two cards in terms of the contract (for example, a personal card and another for a family member), each of the cards with information the respective cardholders are displayed in the list.

Accounts

Accounts opened in terms of the selected contract. It is possible to view the account information in a separate window and to create an adjustment.

Service terms

List of all service terms resulting from the services included in the product, and their values.

Service terms whose definition level is Product and object or Object can edited on this tab.

Limit counters

Service terms of the limit type related to the customer, and their values (in terms of the contract).

Cycle counters

The service terms of the cycle type related to the customer, and their values (in terms of the contract).

IDs

List of the customer identity documents (for example, passport, national identity card or social security card).

Contacts

Contacts details that may be used to communicate with the customer. The contacts can be displayed in a separate window.

Addresses

Address information associated with the customer. An address can be displayed in a separate window.

Notes

Notes to the customer. Each note can be displayed in a separate window. Notes are informational and not used by the system itself. You can add and view a note.

Note: A note cannot be deleted.

Acq.h.

The acquiring hierarchy for the customer objects (for the acquirer’s customers).

Iss.h.

The issuing hierarchy for the customer objects (for the issuer’s customers).

Payment orders

List of the customer payment orders made at the customer’s request.

Templates

Payment order templates.
