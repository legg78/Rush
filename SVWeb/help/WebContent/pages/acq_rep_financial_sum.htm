Data for settlements on commodity/service payment transactions and on cash issue operations

Report name — Data for settlement of trade operations and cash advance operations for SVBO clearing date on payment system for acquirer institution.

Report description — A report is generated on all processed operations in payment systems (Union, Master, Visa, Diners) and acquirer institutions for SVBO clearing date. Used for verification with compensation from payment systems. Intended for Processing Administration ОТОР.

Report header — The data for calculations on operations of payment of the goods / services and on operations of delivery of cash for the period start date - end date on payment system payment system name.
