Financial processing

SmartVista employs the following object types in processing transactions:

•Rule sets, which are executed when an event occurs or a transaction is processed.

•Containers, which may be launched manually, on a defined schedule, or in response to an event.

Financial processing rules determine how settlements are performed between the parties related to a transaction of a particular type. For instance, when a cardholder withdraws cash at a third-party ATM, an issuer account is debited and an acquirer accounts is credited. Additionally, fees are typically charged.
