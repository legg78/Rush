Dispute applications

A dispute application is information about a claim that is submitted on a standard, bank-adopted form, registered in the SmartVista Back Office, and processed according to a predefined workflow. A cardholder can either fill in a dispute form at a bank branch or submit a claim through an alternative channel such as e-mail, phone, Internet banking site, or website feedback forms. Supporting documents can be provided with a dispute applications as attachments.

The process of dispute application handling consists of two stages:

1.Creating a dispute application: At this stage, a user who receives a cardholder's claim (typically a bank branch employee) creates a dispute application and registers it in the system. A new application can be modified or deleted. When the application is complete, it is submitted to the employee responsible for dispute application review (a chargeback team member).

2.Approving or rejecting a dispute application: At this stage, a chargeback team member reviews the application and either accepts or rejects it. After approving the application, a dispute case is created (see Initiating a dispute). A rejected application is returned to the bank branch.
