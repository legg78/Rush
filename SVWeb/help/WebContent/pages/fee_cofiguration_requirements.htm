Fee configuration requirements

This section describes the requirements of the different payment systems that must be applied when creating a fee. There requirements are relevant to specifying fee criteria and configuring fee selection conditions.

Payment system

Requirements

Visa

Visa fees are configured in accordance with the requirements described in the document Visa Europe Interchange Reimbursement Fees, Cash Disbursement Fees and Qualification Criteria Guide.

MasterCard

MasterCard fees are configured in accordance with the requirements described in the document Interchange Manual Europe Region.

China UnionPay

China UnionPay fees are calculated only for POS transactions. There are two categories: fees for refunds and fees for other POS transactions.

For fee calculation, the following parameters are set:

•Transaction type:

▪Refund

▪All other POS transactions

•Institution

•Fee currency:

▪Euro

▪USD

The following fee calculation methods are configured:

•Fixed value

•Percent

American Express

American Express fees are calculated for cash withdrawal transactions only, performed either on an ATM or on a POS terminal located in a branch of the financial institution.

For fee calculation, the following parameters are set:

•Transaction type:

▪ATM cash withdrawal

▪POS cash advance

For other transaction types, fees are not calculated.

•Card type:

▪National card — a card issued by the bank located in the same country as the acquirer’s one.

▪International card — a card issued by the bank located outside acquirer’s country

The card type is defined based on BIN tables provided by American Express.

•Currency

Because settlement within the system is performed in euros, fees calculated in any other currencies are converted to euros using Visa currency exchange.

•Calculation rule:

▪Fixed value

▪Percent

▪Minimum between fix value and percent

Diners Club

Fees for Diners Club are calculated for cash withdrawal transactions performed either on ATMs or POS devices located in a branch of the financial institution.

For fee calculation, the following parameters are set:

•Transaction type:

▪ATM cash withdrawal

▪POS cash advance

For other transaction types, fees are not calculated.

•Currency

Because settlement within the system is performed in euros, fees calculated in any other currencies are converted to euros applying Visa currency exchange.

•Calculation rule:

▪Fixed value

▪Percent

▪Minimum between fix value and percent
