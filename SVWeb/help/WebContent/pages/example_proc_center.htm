Example: setting processing center

Task. Configure the following institutions interaction in networks (for example, Visa and Local network):

•Bank A is a principal and interacts with Bank B directly.

•Bank B is the owner of the host in the payment network (Network 1).

•Bank A, Bank C, and Bank D are within a domestic network (Network 2, owned by Bank D) and the routing is performed between Bank A and Bank C, and between Bank C and Bank D, as depicted in the following diagram:

1.Register the required institutions: Bank A, Bank B, Bank C, Bank D.

2.Register Network 1 and specify its members.

3.Register Network 2 and specify its members.

4.Configure the host of Network 1:

oHost owner: Bank B

oInstitution that is the host consumer: Bank A

oSpecify the communication standards and their versions

oSpecify the communication devices

5.Configure the host of the Network 2:

oHost owner: Bank D

oInstitutions that are the host consumers: Bank A, Bank C

oSpecify the communication standards and their versions

oSpecify the communication devices

The required interbank communication is configured.

After the institutions have been combined into one network settlement types can be configured.
