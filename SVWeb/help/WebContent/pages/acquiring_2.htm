Acquiring

The following diagram shows the interaction among key entities involved in acquiring activities:

 

Entity

Description

Acquirer, institution

The bank or financial institution that manages or owns the terminals used to initiate transactions.

Agent

The acquirer bank branch serving customers. This refers to a branch or a geographical unit of a bank.

Customer

(account owner),

a legal entity

Personal and contact data of the customer. A customer record is composed of personal or corporate information contained in a contract.

A customer may have one or more accounts at the bank.

Contract

Contracts describe the bank-customer relationship in terms of services and products used by the customer.

A customer can have several contracts opened in one agent or in several agents.

Account

The account opened at an acquirer branch bank.

Accounts can be related to any element of acquiring structure (merchant, terminal) in any combination. However any account can only be used within a single contract. If it is assumed that there are settlements on a certain hierarchy level, then the account should be attached to it. Otherwise on processing a transaction on the level (for example, find an account), that transaction will fail.

Accounts keep track of accounting and settlement information for the acquiring entity with which they are associated. The account may be associated with the merchant, or with the terminal, depending on which level settlements are to be made.

Merchant

Merchants are business entities that offer services or products, and accept payments via the terminals in the acquirer network. Merchants are registered using the merchant type level entity in the acquirer contract. The merchant contract is made up of templates that define fees, accounts, and other terminal and merchant parameters used during transaction processing.

Terminal

Terminals are an element of an acquiring network. Any type of terminal (ATM, payment kiosk, or POS) can be used to initiate payments within the acquiring network.

Terminals can be registered for merchants at any level of the acquiring structure.
