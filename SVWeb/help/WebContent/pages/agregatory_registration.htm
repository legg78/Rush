Registration of aggregators and partners

Aggregators and partners are registered as institutions. These institutions are associated with the Organization customer type; the payment hosts (connection parameters and payment parameters) are set at the institution level and service terms are set at the customer level:

•The customer is associated with the aggregator. When the contract between the bank and customer is concluded , the service terms for servicing the aggregator are also set (for example, partner warnings limit)

•The same procedure is used for the partners. The contract is concluded with the customer where the appropriate service terms are specified.

•The payee who provides a certain service is registered as a service provider and is associated with the Person customer type. The contract for the customer is registered and the settlement parameters for service providers are specified within this contract (for example, minimum payment amount or one-time expense limit).

The settlement parameters for service providers (such as the minimum payment amount or one-time expense limit) are set via the issuing product service terms within the service providers’ settlements. The payment parameters depend on the gateway that is used for payments, and are set at the product service terms of mutual settlements.
