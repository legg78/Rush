Specifying fee criteria

Fee criteria determine calculation parameters for the defined interchange fee according to the requirements of the relevant payment system.

1. Click Payment system, select the required IPS (Visa, MasterCard, China Union Pay, Diners Club or American Express) and click Fee criteria.

2. Click Add and specify the values of fee rule parameters in the Add/Edit criteria page. For more information about the parameters, see Fee criteria.

To specify the modifier values, click Wizard and enter the required expressions:

3. Click Save.

After the fee has been created and its parameters configured, you must configure the fee selection conditions.
