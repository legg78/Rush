Adding services to the product

Service terms of servicing plastic cards for individuals and entities are described using issuing products that consists of the corresponding services, specified card and account types.

1.Click Issuing > Configuration > Products.

2.Click Search on the search pane.

3.Click the relevant product on the search result pane.

4.Click the Services details pane tab and click Add.

5.Specify the relevant parameters.

Parameter

Description

Parent

Parent service from which the new service inherits service terms and their values.

Service

The service that must be added to the product of the institution.

Mandatory

Specifies whether the service must always be active and its presence cannot be changed by customer.

Min count

Minimum number of the services in the product. If the Mandatory check box is selected then this value will default to zero (0).

Max count

Maximum number of the services in the product.

Unlimited

Determines whether the number of the services in a contract is limited or not. If selected, the maximum number of services (Max count) cannot be specified.

6.Click Save.

If the current product is a parent product in a hierarchy, the service and attributes linked to it are automatically inherited by its child products. This service is marked with a green arrow () in the child product level.
